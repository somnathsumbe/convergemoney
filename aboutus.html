Who Are We?

We introduced Converge Money with an aim of building the most advanced and feature rich channel that could conjoin an investor to a business. We strongly believe in minimizing the following:

  • Repeated efforts that organisations have to go through to find a potential investor
  • Investment reliability challenges
  • Lack of working/growth capital

At Converge Money we take the experience of our users to a next level by communicating the approach of social lending. This is done by bringing together a vibrant set of industry expertise with varied top-notch technologies that would deliver most extensive money managing solutions thereby creating an ecosystem of growth for both investors and businesses.

Converge Money also has its own analytical “secret sauce” when it comes to assessing risk, thus there is no room for doubt when it comes to connecting our users to a broad spectrum of possibilities and opportunities.
